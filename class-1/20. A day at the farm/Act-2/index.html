Choose the correct words to name the young ones of animals.

A baby hen is a ______.

A baby hen is a chick.

A baby dog is a ______.

A baby dog is a puppy.

A baby goat is a _______.

A baby goat is a kid.

A baby sheep is a ________.

A baby sheep is a lamb.

A baby goose is a ________.

A baby goose is a gosling.

A baby cow is a _________.

A baby cow is a calf.
